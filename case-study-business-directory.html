Local Business Directory SaaS

Comprehensive business directory platform with subscription-based revenue model

Home / Portfolio / Local Business Directory SaaS

The Challenge

Cambridgeshire entrepreneurs and small businesses were struggling to gain online visibility and connect with local customers. The challenges identified included:

  • Fragmented online presence across multiple platforms
  • High costs of traditional advertising and marketing
  • Difficulty for customers to find local businesses and services
  • Lack of a centralized platform for Cambridgeshire businesses
  • Limited digital marketing expertise among small business owners
  • Need for a sustainable revenue model for the platform

The opportunity was to create a comprehensive, user-friendly directory that would benefit both businesses and customers while generating recurring revenue.

Our SaaS Solution

We developed a full-featured business directory platform with subscription-based pricing, designed specifically for the Cambridgeshire market:

Core Platform Features:

  • Business Listings: Comprehensive profiles with photos, descriptions, and contact information
  • Category Management: Organized by industry, location, and services offered
  • Search & Filtering: Advanced search with location-based results
  • Review System: Customer reviews and ratings for businesses
  • Event Listings: Local events and business promotions
  • Mobile Responsive: Optimized for all devices and screen sizes

Subscription Tiers:

  • Basic (£15/month): Standard listing with contact details
  • Professional (£35/month): Enhanced listing with photos and description
  • Premium (£65/month): Featured placement, analytics, and promotional tools

Development Process

Conducted thorough testing, onboarded initial businesses, launched marketing campaigns, and provided ongoing support and optimization.

Technical Implementation

Backend Architecture:

  • Django Framework: Robust Python web framework for rapid development
  • PostgreSQL Database: Reliable relational database for complex queries
  • Django REST Framework: API development for mobile and third-party integrations
  • Celery: Asynchronous task processing for email and notifications
  • Redis: Caching and session management for improved performance

Frontend & UX:

  • Bootstrap 5: Responsive design framework
  • JavaScript/jQuery: Interactive features and AJAX functionality
  • Google Maps API: Location-based search and mapping
  • Progressive Web App: Mobile-first design with offline capabilities

Payment & Subscription Management:

  • Stripe Integration: Secure payment processing and subscription management
  • Automated Billing: Recurring payments and invoice generation
  • Dunning Management: Automated retry logic for failed payments
  • Proration: Automatic calculations for plan upgrades/downgrades

SEO & Marketing Features:

  • SEO-optimized URLs and meta tags
  • XML sitemaps and structured data markup
  • Google Analytics and Search Console integration
  • Email marketing automation with Mailchimp
  • Social media integration and sharing

Key Success Factors

What Made This Project Successful:

  • Local Focus: Targeting a specific geographic area created strong community engagement
