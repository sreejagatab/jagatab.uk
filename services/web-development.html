❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical web development project take?

Simple websites take 4-6 weeks, while complex web applications can take 8-16 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase and keep you updated throughout development.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, we offer various maintenance packages including security updates, content updates, performance monitoring, and feature enhancements. All projects include 3 months of free support.

Can you work with our existing hosting and domain?

Absolutely. We can work with any hosting provider and domain registrar. We can also recommend optimal hosting solutions based on your specific requirements and budget.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

All our websites are built mobile-first and are fully responsive. We test on multiple devices and screen sizes to ensure optimal performance across all platforms.

Can you integrate with our existing business systems?

Yes, we specialize in integrations with CRM systems, payment processors, inventory management, email marketing platforms, and other business tools.

Do you provide training on how to update the website?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training sessions and documentation. We can also set up content management systems that make updates easy for non-technical users.

What about SEO and search engine visibility?

All our websites are built with SEO best practices, including proper meta tags, structured data, fast loading times, and mobile optimization. We can also provide ongoing SEO services.
