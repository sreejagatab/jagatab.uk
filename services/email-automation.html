❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Which email platforms do you work with?

We work with all major platforms including Mailchimp, ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, HubSpot, and can set up custom SMTP solutions. We'll recommend the best platform for your specific needs and budget.

How quickly can email automation be set up?

Basic automation can be set up within 1-2 weeks, while complex multi-sequence campaigns typically take 3-4 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.

Can you help with email deliverability issues?

Yes, we optimize for deliverability including domain authentication, list hygiene, content optimization, and reputation management to ensure your emails reach the inbox.

Do you provide email copywriting services?

Absolutely. We create compelling email copy that converts, including subject lines, body content, and call-to-action optimization based on your brand voice and audience.

How do you measure email automation success?

We track open rates, click-through rates, conversion rates, revenue attribution, list growth, and ROI. You'll receive detailed monthly reports with actionable insights.

Can you integrate with our existing sales process?

Yes, we design email automation to complement your sales funnel, including lead scoring, sales team notifications, and automated handoffs at the right moments.
