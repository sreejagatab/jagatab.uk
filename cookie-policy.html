Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and tracking technologies on our website

Last Updated: December 2024

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes across our AI automation, Python development, and Virtual Assistant services:

  • Essential website functionality
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Marketing and advertising (with your consent)
  • Personalizing your experience
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Service preference tracking for Virtual Assistant consultations
  • Session management for client portals and dashboards

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our website may contain third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics and performance tracking
  • Google Tag Manager: Tag and script management
  • Facebook Pixel: Advertising and conversion tracking
  • LinkedIn Insight Tag: Professional network advertising
  • Hotjar: User behavior analysis and heatmaps

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

5.1 Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner allowing you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Decline non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences

5.2 Browser Settings

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

5.3 Opt-Out Links

You can opt out of specific tracking services:

  • Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Facebook Ad Preferences
  • LinkedIn Opt-out

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect your experience:

  • Some website features may not work properly
  • We won't be able to remember your preferences
  • Analytics data will be incomplete
  • Personalized content may not be available

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We'll notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and posting a notice on our website.

8. Data Protection

Cookie data is processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws, including UK GDPR. We implement appropriate security measures to protect cookie data from unauthorized access or misuse.
