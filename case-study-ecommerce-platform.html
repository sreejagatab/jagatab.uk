The Challenge

Cambridgeshire Outdoor Gear, a local retailer specializing in hiking and camping equipment, needed to transition from a brick-and-mortar store to a competitive online presence. Their challenges included:

  • Outdated website with poor user experience and no e-commerce functionality
  • Zero online sales despite having quality products and local reputation
  • Poor search engine visibility for relevant product keywords
  • Competition from large online retailers like Amazon and specialized outdoor stores
  • Limited digital marketing knowledge and resources
  • Need for inventory management integration with existing systems

The business needed a complete digital transformation to compete online while maintaining their local customer base and expertise.

Our E-commerce Solution

We developed a comprehensive e-commerce platform built on WordPress/WooCommerce with advanced SEO optimization and custom features:

E-commerce Features:

  • Product Catalog: Comprehensive product management with categories, variations, and inventory tracking
  • Shopping Cart & Checkout: Streamlined checkout process with multiple payment options
  • Customer Accounts: User registration, order history, and wishlist functionality
  • Inventory Management: Real-time stock tracking with low-stock alerts
  • Order Management: Automated order processing and fulfillment workflows
  • Mobile Optimization: Fully responsive design for all devices

SEO Optimization:

  • Technical SEO: Optimized site structure, loading speeds, and mobile performance
  • Product SEO: Optimized product pages with schema markup and rich snippets
  • Content Strategy: SEO-optimized category pages and buying guides
  • Local SEO: Google My Business optimization and local search targeting
  • Link Building: Strategic partnerships with outdoor blogs and local businesses
